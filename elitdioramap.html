Description

The diorama is of the mother's apartment in Zion Mansion, as described in the play Boom (by Jean Tay).

No specific scene was chosen but the act of mother and Boon eating was to show that they were consuming bak kut teh and that they were in a conversation with one another, regarding the topic of the en bloc sale and selling of the house.

Themes portrayed:
1. the conflicting pursuits and views of the younger and the older generation
2. memory
