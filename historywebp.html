Description

In this website, we explored how the life of students and the education system in Singapore have transformed since independence. We dived into six different aspects, namely
1. teaching methods and discipline
2. examinations and scoring system
3. school facilities and teaching instruments
4. programmes provided
5. ECAs / CCAs available
6. leisure activities students do in their free time
